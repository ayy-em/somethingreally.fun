How (and where) do I even begin?

An absence of choice is sometimes better than having way too many good options. You're probably motivated by hype, by great career opportunities and by having a burning desire to have less people to talk to at work. But once you sit down to make a plan, everything suddenly becomes overwhelming. Developer? Frontend? DevOps? Backend? Android or iOS? Software engineering?
Get used to it! Being confused and overwhelmed is gonna be your go-to state most of the time if you're ever motivated enough to stick to it. However, I do assure you that it's worth it and it's definitely doable.
